[Slide 2: The Problem]
# The Problem: The ATS Keyword Tax
- **Broken Funnel**: Traditional Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) rely on basic keyword matching.
- **Lost Talent**: Brilliant candidates get filtered out due to resume syntax variations (e.g., "PyTorch" vs "Deep Learning").
- **Manual Overhead**: Experienced recruiters spend 40+ hours per week manually vetting matches to bypass poor automated filtration.
- **Objective Void**: Absence of granular, explainable metrics to assess matching decisions.

---

[Slide 3: Industry Challenges]
# Key Challenges in Modern Recruiting
1. **Semantic Ambiguity**: Distinguishing candidate roles (e.g., leading a project vs. participating).
2. **Resume Over-optimization**: Candidates "keyword-stuffing" resumes to trick primitive parsers.
3. **Multidimensional Profiles**: Integrating technical skills, years of experience, degree prestige, and open-source contributions.
4. **Explainability Deficit**: Deep learning black boxes ranking profiles without verifiable reasoning.

---

[Slide 4: Our Solution]
# The Solution: TalentMind-AI
- **Dense Representation**: Encoding profiles and job descriptions into shared semantic vectors using Sentence Transformers.
- **FAISS-Powered Search**: Real-time retrieval indexing utilizing fast inner-product similarity spaces.
- **Configurable Scoring Fusion**: Multi-criteria matching blending semantic alignment, skills density, experience tenure, and behavioral metrics.
- **Cognitive Refinement**: Optional LLM-assisted re-ranking (Gemini 3.5 Flash) to parse complex project summaries.
- **Explainable AI (XAI)**: Diagnostic natural language reasons accompanying every ranked profile.

---

[Slide 5: Architecture Diagram]
# System Architecture
- **Inbound Layers**: Unstructured Job Description & Candidate Resumes.
- **Parser Core**: Rule-based NLP and Gemini Cognitive Extractors.
- **Embeddings Layer**: `sentence-transformers` generating high-dimensional vectors.
- **Retrieval Engine**: FAISS flat index matching candidates at scale.
- **Aggregation Logic**: Composite weighted scoring fusion.
- **Explanatory Tier**: Dynamic, automated rationale compilers.

---

[Slide 6: Semantic Retrieval & Embedding Pipeline]
# Semantic Retrieval Pipeline
- **Representation Model**: `all-MiniLM-L6-v2` (384-dimensional dense embeddings).
- **Index Optimization**: FAISS IndexFlatIP (Inner Product) to capture cosine similarities.
- **Mathematical Alignment**: 
  $$\text{Semantic Similarity} = \frac{\vec{JD} \cdot \vec{Cand}}{\|\vec{JD}\| \|\vec{Cand}\|}$$
- **Result**: Immediate retrieval of candidates sharing conceptual context rather than simple keyword intersects.

---

[Slide 7: Multi-Criteria Scoring Formula]
# Hybrid Scoring Fusion Formula
The overall score fuses six distinct candidate compatibility dimensions:

- **Semantic Similarity**: 40% (Shared vector contexts)
- **Technical/Soft Skills**: 20% (Intersection-over-union density)
- **Years of Experience**: 15% (Log-scaled seniority alignment)
- **Education Level**: 10% (Degree prestige and domain fit)
- **Behavioral Signals**: 10% (Platform commits, hackathons, activity)
- **Profile Completeness**: 5% (Data depth verification)

*All weights are fully customizable by recruiters.*

[Slide 8: Explainable AI & Human-in-the-Loop]
# Explainable AI (XAI)
- **Verifiable Matching**: No black-box decisions; every ranked profile lists explicit rationales.
- **Contextual Anchors**: 
  - *"Meets experience standard with 6.5 years in comparable roles."*

  - *"Excellent matching density for PyTorch, NLP, and Docker competencies."*
- **Empowerment**: Recruiters can auditing the decision pathway instantly, reinforcing trust.
