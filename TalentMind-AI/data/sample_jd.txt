Job Title: Senior AI & Deep Learning Research Engineer
Domain: Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
Department: Cognitive Research Lab
Experience Required: 5+ Years

- Containerize models and APIs using Docker and FastAPI for integration with backend microservices on AWS.
